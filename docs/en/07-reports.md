# Reports & Analysis

The Reports section lets you analyze your finances — by month, by year, by category.

---

## Navigation

Under **Reports** you'll find two tabs:

- **Monthly Report** — detailed view for a single month
- **Yearly Report** — overview of all 12 months of a year

---

## Monthly Report

Select the month and year at the top. The report shows:

### Income & Expense Overview

A table with all active bookings in the selected month:

| Income | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Salary | $3,500 |
| Child benefit | $300 |
| **Total** | **$3,800** |

| Expenses | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Rent | $1,200 |
| Electricity/Gas | $150 |
| Internet | $60 |
| Groceries | $480 |
| Loan payments | $350 |
| **Total** | **$2,240** |

| | |
|--|--|
| **Free Money** | **$1,560** |

### Category Donut Chart

A pie chart shows how your expenses are distributed across categories:

- 🔵 Housing: 50%
- 🟢 Groceries: 20%
- 🟡 Transportation: 16%
- 🔴 Leisure: 14%

You can see at a glance where most of your money goes.

![Category Donut Chart](../images/category-donut.png)

### Active Loans

Loans are shown as a separate expense block in the monthly report.

## Yearly Report

The yearly report shows all 12 months side by side in a table:

|  | Jan | Feb | Mar | Apr | … | Dec | **Year** |
|--|-----|-----|-----|-----|---|-----|---------|
| Income | 3,800 | 3,800 | 4,100 | 4,100 | … | 4,100 | **48,000** |
| Expenses | 2,240 | 2,240 | 2,240 | 2,240 | … | 2,240 | **26,880** |
| Free | 1,560 | 1,560 | 1,860 | 1,860 | … | 1,860 | **21,120** |

> ✅ In March you can see the effect of the salary raise — HELFINANCE correctly reflects scheduled changes over time.

## Export

Both reports can be exported:

| Format | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **PDF** | Print-ready view of the report, ideal for records |
| **CSV** | Spreadsheet format for Excel, LibreOffice, or Google Sheets |

Click **Export** in the top right and choose the format.

---

## Filters & Time Periods

- **Choose month/year**: Dropdown at the top of the report
- Monthly report: Single month
- Yearly report: Full calendar year

---

## Category Analysis

In the monthly report, alongside the donut chart, you'll also see a category details table:

| Category | Bookings | Total | Share |
|----------|---------|-------|-------|
| Housing | 3 | $1,200 | 50% |
| Groceries | 1 | $480 | 20% |
| Transportation | 2 | $380 | 16% |
| Leisure | 2 | $300 | 14% |

**What you learn from this:**
- Which category consumes the most budget?
- Where is there potential for savings?
- Which categories are fixed vs. adjustable?

---

## Tips for Analysis

**Monthly comparison**

Compare the same month in the yearly report year over year. Have expenses increased? New fixed costs added?

**Spotting category bloat**

If one category dominates the donut at 50%+, it's worth breaking it down further or questioning spending in that category.

**Year-end planning**

Use the yearly report at the end of the year to plan for the next:
- Which expenses can be reduced?
- Which contracts are expiring?
- How has the Health Score developed over the year?
