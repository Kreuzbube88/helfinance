# Reports & Analysis

The Reports section lets you analyze your finances — by month, by category, over time.

---

## Navigation

Under **Reports** you'll find three tabs:

- **Overview** — Hero metrics, donut chart, required savings summary
- **Details** — Income and expense tables, full required savings breakdown
- **History** — 6-month bar chart and snapshot archive

---

## Month Navigation

At the top you'll find navigation buttons to switch months:

- **← Previous** — go to the previous month
- Month name and year in the center
- **Next →** — go to the next month
- **Today** — jump to the current month

---

## Tab: Overview

Shows three hero cards and two cards below:

| Hero Card | Content |
|-----------|---------|
| **Income** | Sum of all income in the selected month |
| **Expenses** | Sum of all expenses in the selected month |
| **Balance** | Income minus expenses |

Below that:

- **Donut Chart** — expenses visualized by category
- **Required Savings** — shows up to 5 of the most important irregular expenses with their monthly reserve amount

---

---

## Tab: History

- **6-month bar chart** — income, expenses, and balance for the last 6 months compared side by side
- **Snapshot archive** — saved monthly snapshots for reference

---

## Yearly Report

The yearly report shows all 12 months side by side in a table:

|  | Jan | Feb | Mar | Apr | … | Dec | **Year** |
|--|-----|-----|-----|-----|---|-----|---------|
| Income | 3,800 | 3,800 | 4,100 | 4,100 | … | 4,100 | **48,000** |
| Expenses | 2,240 | 2,240 | 2,240 | 2,240 | … | 2,240 | **26,880** |
| Free | 1,560 | 1,560 | 1,860 | 1,860 | … | 1,860 | **21,120** |

> ✅ In March you can see the effect of the salary raise — HELFINANCE correctly reflects scheduled changes over time.

## Export

Both reports can be exported:

| Format | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **PDF** | Print-ready view of the report, ideal for records |
| **CSV** | Spreadsheet format for Excel, LibreOffice, or Google Sheets |

Click **Export** in the top right and choose the format.

---

## Tips for Analysis

**Monthly comparison**

Compare the same month in the yearly report year over year. Have expenses increased? New fixed costs added?

**Spotting category bloat**

If one category dominates the donut at 50%+, it's worth breaking it down further or questioning spending in that category.

**Year-end planning**

Use the yearly report at the end of the year to plan for the next:
- Which expenses can be reduced?
- Which contracts are expiring?
- How has the Health Score developed over the year?
