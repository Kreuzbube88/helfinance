# Savings & Emergency Fund

The Savings section helps you keep track of your savings account balance and plan for irregular expenses.

> ⚠️ **Note:** HELFINANCE is not a savings goals system. You cannot define goals like "save $10,000 by end of 2026." It's about tracking your current balance and understanding how much you should set aside each month.

---

## Setting the Opening Balance

On your first visit to the Savings section, enter your current savings account balance:

1. Click **Set Opening Balance**
2. Enter the current amount in your savings account (e.g. $4,000)
3. Choose the date (usually today)
4. Save

> ✅ This is the starting point. All future transactions will be calculated based on this amount.

---

## Recording Transactions

### Deposit (Adding Money to Savings)

When you transfer money to your savings account:

1. Click **+ Deposit**
2. Enter a description (e.g. "Monthly savings transfer")
3. Enter the amount (e.g. $250)
4. Choose the date
5. Save

### Withdrawal (Taking Money from Savings)

When you take money from your savings account:

1. Click **+ Withdrawal**
2. Enter a description (e.g. "Washing machine broke down")
3. Enter the amount (e.g. $550)
4. Choose the date
5. Save

### Transaction History

All deposits and withdrawals are displayed in a table:

| Date | Description | Amount | Balance |
|------|-------------|--------|---------|
| 03/01/2025 | Opening Balance | +$4,000 | $4,000 |
| 04/01/2025 | Monthly savings | +$250 | $4,250 |
| 04/15/2025 | Washing machine | −$550 | $3,700 |

## 12-Month Overview

A bar chart shows your balance for each of the past 12 months. At a glance you can see:

- Trend: Is your balance rising or falling?
- Outliers: Months where large withdrawals were made
- Progress: Is your emergency fund growing?

## Irregular Expenses

In the lower section of the Savings tab you'll see a special list: **All expenses that don't occur monthly** — quarterly, semi-annual, and yearly bookings.

**Example:**

| Expense | Amount | Interval | Set aside per month |
|---------|--------|----------|---------------------|
| Car registration | $240 | Yearly | $20.00 |
| Home insurance | $120 | Yearly | $10.00 |
| Dental checkup | $150 | Semi-annual | $25.00 |
| **Total** | | | **$55.00** |

> ✅ This list answers the question: *"How much do I need to set aside monthly so I'm not caught off guard?"*

## Emergency Fund Recommendation

HELFINANCE automatically calculates a recommended minimum amount for your emergency fund:

**Rule of thumb:** 3–6 months of monthly expenses

**Example:**

```
Monthly expenses:         $1,900
Recommendation (3 months): $5,700
Recommendation (6 months): $11,400
```

**Why is this important?**

An emergency fund protects you from unexpected expenses:
- Car repair: $800
- Dentist: $600
- Job loss: several months of living expenses

Without savings, you'd have to rely on credit — which gets expensive fast.

### Dashboard Warning

If your current savings balance is below the recommended minimum, a warning appears on the dashboard:

> ⚠️ "Your savings are below the recommended emergency fund (3 months = $5,700). Current: $2,500."

Your savings balance feeds into the Dashboard Health Score. Having a solid emergency fund improves your Health Score.
